Delicious Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 250g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef to the skillet. Cook until browned.
  4. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15 minutes.
  5. Combine the sauce with the spaghetti and serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.
