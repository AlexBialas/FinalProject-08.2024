Chicken with Pomegranate Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken over medium heat for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.
  3. While chicken is cooking, prepare the salad by mixing mixed greens, pomegranate seeds, feta cheese, red onion, and walnuts in a large bowl.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Pour over the salad and toss gently.
  5. Once chicken is cooked, slice it and place it on top of the salad. Garnish with fresh parsley and enjoy!
