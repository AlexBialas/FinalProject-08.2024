Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans (14.5 ounces each) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the diced tomatoes (with their juice) and vegetable broth.
  5. Add the dried basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and let cook for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Using an immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth. (Alternatively, carefully transfer to a blender in batches.)
  7. If desired, stir in the heavy cream for a richer flavor.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. Enjoy!
